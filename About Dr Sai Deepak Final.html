About Dr Sai Deepak

Dr Sai Deepak is a consultant neurophysician at Citi Neuro Centre, Miyapur, Hyderabad with more than 10 years of experience in Neurology. He completed his MBBS from Gandhi Medical College, Secunderabad. Following this, he worked as a resident doctor in Neurology in the prestigious National Institute of Mental Health and Neurosciences (NIMHANS), Bangalore for five years. In 2017, he received the Anisya Vasant memorial award for the best outgoing resident in Neurology, NIMHANS.

He worked as a post DM senior resident in the prestigious Nizams Institute of Medical Sciences (NIMS), Hyderabad. He has a vast experience of working in top corporate hospitals including Citi Neuro Centre, AIG hospitals, Yashoda and Srikara Hospitals in Hyderabad.

Dr Sai Deepak has received advanced training in Parkinson's disease and Movement disorders (as a fellow) from Citi Neuro centre, Hyderabad where he currently works as a consultant. His areas of expertise include Parkinson's disease, Movement disorders, Deep Brain Stimulation (DBS), Botulinum toxin (Botox) injection, Headache, Stroke, Seizures, Spinal problems, Weakness, Paresthesias, Sensory neurological issues, Memory and Cognitive disorders and other neurological illnesses.

Dr Sai Deepak is an astute clinician, treating his patients with the highest standard of care and with empathy. Apart from patient care, he is actively involved in research into neurological illnesses. His areas of research include Parkinson's disease, Movement disorders, Seizures, Sleep, Peripheral nerve and Pediatric neurological illnesses.
